We have a vision: within the next couple years, Big Tech won’t be the default way to connect online. Instead, there will be a new ecosystem of apps and sites, ones that are open and interoperable but also personal and contextual. The next generation of the web will give users agency over their data and their experiences online.

That’s why we’re calling this project habitat. Humans have built homes for themselves for thousands of years, and we deserve to have that capability in our digital spaces too.

Finally, this future will be built together, not in proprietary silos. That’s why we’re building on AT protocol. Learn more about what we’re building here, and sign up for our beta wait list.
