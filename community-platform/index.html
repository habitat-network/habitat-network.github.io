Your community's data should belong to you.

Habitat's Community Platform is for groups that are ready to own their data and build for their unique needs, instead of using mass-produced software. Habitat gives you the foundation to reduce reliance on Big Tech, build custom apps, and collaborate with other communities.

Build for your community's needs; let Habitat handle the rest.

With Habitat as your data backend, you can create custom frontends and apps tailored to your community's needs. Rather than paying for SaaS tools only to use one feature from each, build directly for your community's needs in an integrated UI. Get in touch to learn how.

Open-source, open protocols, deployed anywhere.

Habitat is fully open source and built on AT protocol, an open protocol for data portability and interoperability. Run it on our hosted service, deploy it to your own infrastructure, or even self-host. Your community's data is your, forever.

Built-in primitives.

Community members can login with their existing identities--Google or AT Protocol--so they don't need another password. We offer fine-grained permissioning APIs, admin app authorization, and developer convenience functions, so you can focus on your community's experience while Habitat handles the infra.
